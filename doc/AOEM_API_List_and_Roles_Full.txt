AOEM API LIST & ROLE PERMISSIONS (FULL ROLE NAMES)

ROLES:
- Admin: Quản trị viên hệ thống, toàn quyền.
- SC Staff: Nhân viên trung tâm dịch vụ (Service Center Staff).
- SC Technician: Kỹ thuật viên trung tâm (Service Center Technician).
- EVM Staff: Nhân viên hãng sản xuất (OEM / EVM Staff).

=== AUTHENTICATION & USER MANAGEMENT ===
POST /api/auth/login                     → Admin✅ | SC Staff✅ | SC Technician✅ | EVM Staff✅
GET  /api/auth/me                        → Admin✅ | SC Staff✅ | SC Technician✅ | EVM Staff✅
POST /api/users                          → Admin✅ | SC Staff❌ | SC Technician❌ | EVM Staff❌
GET  /api/users                          → Admin✅ | SC Staff❌ | SC Technician❌ | EVM Staff❌
PUT  /api/users/{id}                     → Admin✅ | SC Staff❌ | SC Technician❌ | EVM Staff❌
